Cost of Study in Denmark

Tuition Fees

  • EU/EEA Students Free at public universities
  • Non-EU Students (Undergraduate) €6,000–€16,000/year
  • Non-EU Students (Master’s) €8,000–€21,000/year

Living Expenses

  • Accommodation €5,400–€9,600/year
  • Food & Groceries €2,400–€3,600/year
  • Transportation €600–€1,200/year
  • Health Insurance €200–€400/year (non-EU)

Visa Process for Denmark

Student Residence Permit Requirements

  • 1
    Admission letter from a Danish institution
  • 2
    Proof of financial support (DKK 6,397/month, ~€860)
  • 3
    Valid passport
  • 4
    Health insurance covering Denmark
  • 5
    Application via Danish embassy or online portal
